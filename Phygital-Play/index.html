The Subscription Trap — Solved

The simulation identified that Year 2 subscription renewal faces student resistance. Two solutions were validated:

✓ Free Coffee Bundle

Pay $4.99 Year 2 renewal → instantly receive a free medium coffee at Brown Coffee (~$3.50 value). The subscription is no longer a "server tax."

✓ Seasonal Content Calendar

Water Festival boat racing animations, Khmer New Year traditional cat outfits. Content fatigue set in by Month 3 — now we have a content pipeline.

"The simulation highlighted a classic 'High Margin, Low Retention' pattern. Excellent upfront cash margins (78.9%) and favorable server economics ($0.12/user/year), but novelty declines by Month 3 without fresh content. The recommended action plan addresses all identified risks." — Mirofish Simulation Report
