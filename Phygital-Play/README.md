## 📋 Overview

A "Phygital" (Physical + Digital) virtual pet ecosystem launching in **Phnom Penh, Cambodia**. Users adopt a digital cat that lives on a shared map of the city, follows their real-world GPS location, and reacts to where they go — sipping coffee at cafes, studying at universities, shopping at malls.
